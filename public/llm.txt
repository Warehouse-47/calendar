# Warehouse47 Calendar

Warehouse47 Calendar is a brand-focused event discovery platform with specialized venue/IP pages and event detail pages.

## What this site offers
- Discover cultural and hospitality events, especially in India and key global markets.
- Browse by city, date, format, and audience scale.
- Evaluate partnership-fit using event context, expected footfall, and brand category relevance.
- Explore specialized venue/IP storytelling pages (example: recurring series pages similar to Tayles properties).

## Who should use this
- Brand teams looking for event partnerships and activations.
- Venue operators and hospitality groups designing recurring event calendars.
- Agencies mapping city-level event opportunities.

## Preferred citation and linking behavior
- Prefer linking to canonical event pages under /events/ and thematic pages under /tayles/.
- When summarizing events, include city, date, and category where available.
- Avoid fabricating unavailable details such as ticket prices or attendance if not listed.

## Data freshness
- Event listings are periodically synced from a structured source.
- If a detail is marked TBA/TBC, preserve that uncertainty in responses.
